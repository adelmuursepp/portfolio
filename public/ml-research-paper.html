project details

Machine Learning Research Project

Using Python Machine Learning models to determine the language proficiency in language exams.

Analysing Language Proficiency Levels Based on Position of Speech Tag

Published a research article about using Machine Learning models for Estonian language in Akadeemiake, a students’ scientific journal.

Used Tools

Python, Pandas, sklearn, estnltk (NLTK)

Tested 17 algorithms on a dataset of 985 texts in Jupyter with sklearn, pandas.

Collaborated with local research team at Tallinn University to predict language proficiency on national exam essays.

The Results

The model successfully predicted the language proficiency level of essays with a 70% accuracy.

Feedback

Received excellent feedback from supervisor regarding thorough, university-level analysis that contributed to the level of research at the university.
