Infosõlm Podcast

Infosõlm podcast is the first students led foreign politics podcast in Estonia. Founded by me and Laura Sofie Tohver.

Mission

Represent students' perspective on world affairs – NATO, the EU, tension with Russian and much more

Prime Minister as the guest

Interviewed the Prime Minister of Estonia, former Foreign Minister, former Head of States for the International Criminal Court

Website for Podcast

Most often the podcast was listened to on the website.

I integrated Buzzspout API to automatically download new episodes so people would have access to our episodes.

Over 2000 downloads

Although we got over 4000 downloads overall, over half of them were from people visiting the website
